Who We Are

Founded by two friends, Natalie Wong and Natalie Vidarte, our website is a labor of love born from a shared passion for crocheting and a desire to spread the joy of this timeless craft.

Natalie Wong and Natalie Vidarte, came together with a vision to create a welcoming space for crocheters of all levels. With backgrounds in teaching and design, they are dedicated to making the world of crochet accessible and enjoyable for everyone.



What We Do

From beginner workshops to advanced patterns, Twices as Nice Crochet offers a wide range of resources to suit every skill level. Whether you're picking up a hook for the first time or looking for your next challenging project, you'll find something to inspire you here.
