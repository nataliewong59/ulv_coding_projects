Supplies

Home / Supplies

Some must needs to start crocheting!

Whether you are a beginner or advanced here are some useful tools to crochet!

Crochet Hooks

These come in various sizes, each suited for different yarn weights. They are the primary tool used to create crochet stitches.

Yarn

Choose yarn in the weight and color you prefer for your project. Worsted weight yarn is a good choice for beginners as it's versatile and easy to work with.

Scissors

You'll need scissors for cutting yarn.

Yarn Needles or Tapestry Needles

These are used for weaving in ends and sewing pieces together.

Stitch Markers

These help mark your stitches or rows, especially in more complex patterns.

Measuring Tape

Useful for checking gauge and measuring your work.

Patterns

Whether it's a printed pattern, a digital download, or a pattern from a book, having clear instructions is crucial. Thats why we offer the best Patterns! For advanced and beginners alike!

Crochet Bag or Basket

A bag or basket to store your yarn and projects keeps everything organized and portable.

Refrence Materials

Having a crochet stitch dictionary or guidebook handy can be useful for learning new stitches and techniques.
